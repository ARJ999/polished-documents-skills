# Deloitte Brand Guide

## Overview
Deloitte is one of the "Big Four" professional services networks. Their visual identity balances professional credibility with modern approachability through distinctive teal-blue tones.

## Visual Identity

### Typography
- **Headings**: Calibri (sans-serif) - Bold weight
- **Body**: Calibri (sans-serif) - Regular weight
- **Characteristic**: Clean, modern sans-serif throughout

### Color Palette
| Color | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| Deloitte Teal | #007CB0 | Primary accent, H1/H2 headings |
| Teal Blue | #0076A8 | Secondary accents |
| Black | #000000 | Body text, H3 headings |
| Mid Gray | #666666 | Captions, secondary text |
| White | #FFFFFF | Background |

### Design Principles
- Modern professional aesthetic
- Rounded corners (8px border-radius)
- Teal color as signature element
- Balanced whitespace
- Accessible and approachable

## Best Used For
- Audit reports
- Risk assessments
- Compliance documentation
- Advisory reports
- Professional services proposals
- Due diligence reports

## Style Specifications
```
H1: Calibri 40pt, #007CB0 (Teal), Bold
H2: Calibri 28pt, #007CB0 (Teal), Bold
H3: Calibri 16pt, #000000 (Black), Bold
Body: Calibri 11pt, #000000
Caption: Calibri 9pt, #666666
```

## Document Characteristics
- Rounded table corners
- Headers and callouts accent usage
- Modern, approachable feel
- Clean sans-serif typography
- Professional yet friendly tone
