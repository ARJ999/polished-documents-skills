# IDBI Bank Brand Guide

## Overview
IDBI Bank (Industrial Development Bank of India) is a leading development and commercial banking institution established in 1964, serving as a catalyst for India's industrial and economic growth. The brand combines institutional trust with progressive energy through a distinctive green and orange color palette.

## Visual Identity

### Typography
- **Headings**: Calibri (sans-serif) - Bold weight for modern authority
- **Body**: Calibri (sans-serif) - Clean, professional readability
- **Characteristic**: Unified sans-serif system for clarity and accessibility

### Color Palette

| Color | Hex | RGB | Usage |
|-------|-----|-----|-------|
| Viridian Green | #00836C | R:0 G:131 B:108 | Primary brand color, H1 headings, key accents |
| Princeton Orange | #F58220 | R:245 G:130 B:32 | Secondary accent, highlights, call-to-action |
| Deep Teal | #1A3A34 | R:26 G:58 B:52 | H2/H3 headings, body text |
| Slate Gray | #4A5568 | R:74 G:85 B:104 | Secondary text, captions |
| White | #FFFFFF | R:255 G:255 B:255 | Background |

### Design Principles
- **Trust**: Institutional credibility and reliability
- **Progress**: Forward-thinking development focus
- **Clarity**: Clean, accessible, government-quality documentation
- **Authority**: Commanding presence in financial communications
- Subtle rounded corners (4px border radius) for approachable professionalism

## Best Used For
- Financial reports and statements
- Government and regulatory documents
- Development proposals and project documentation
- Banking communications and client materials
- Annual reports and investor documentation
- Institutional presentations
- Policy documents and white papers

## Style Specifications
```
H1: Calibri 36pt, #00836C (Viridian Green), Bold
H2: Calibri 28pt, #1A3A34 (Deep Teal), Bold
H3: Calibri 18pt, #1A3A34 (Deep Teal), Bold
Body: Calibri 11pt, #1A3A34
Caption: Calibri 9pt, #4A5568
```

## Document Characteristics
- Professional banking table style with green accents
- Headers and highlights use viridian green
- Orange accents for important callouts and CTAs
- Clean, institutional aesthetic
- Subtle rounded corners throughout
- Government-grade documentation quality
- Trust and development messaging

## Brand Voice
- **Formal**: Appropriate for institutional communication
- **Authoritative**: Commands confidence in financial matters
- **Progressive**: Reflects development-focused mission
- **Accessible**: Clear language for diverse audiences
