# KPMG Brand Guide

## Overview
KPMG is a global professional services firm and one of the "Big Four." Their visual identity projects corporate authority through bold blue tones and substantial typography.

## Visual Identity

### Typography
- **Headings**: Calibri (sans-serif) - Bold weight
- **Body**: Calibri (sans-serif) - Regular weight
- **Characteristic**: Large, bold headings with corporate gravitas

### Color Palette
| Color | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| KPMG Blue | #005EB8 | Primary accent, buttons |
| KPMG Dark Blue | #00338D | Headings, primary text |
| Mid Gray | #666666 | Captions, secondary text |
| White | #FFFFFF | Background |

### Design Principles
- Bold corporate presence
- Two-tone blue palette
- Slightly rounded corners (3px)
- Large, impactful headings
- Professional authority

## Best Used For
- Financial reports
- Compliance documentation
- Governance reports
- Regulatory filings
- Tax documentation
- Assurance reports

## Style Specifications
```
H1: Calibri 42pt, #00338D (Dark Blue), Bold
H2: Calibri 38pt, #00338D, Bold
H3: Calibri 16pt, #00338D, Bold
Body: Calibri 11pt, #00338D
Caption: Calibri 9pt, #666666
```

## Document Characteristics
- Rounded modern table style
- Buttons and badges accent usage
- Strong corporate identity
- Consistent blue throughout
- Authority and trust signals
