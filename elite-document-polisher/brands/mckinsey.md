# McKinsey & Company Brand Guide

## Overview
McKinsey & Company is the world's most prestigious management consulting firm. Their visual identity projects executive authority, strategic thinking, and uncompromising professionalism.

## Visual Identity

### Typography
- **Headings**: Georgia (serif) - Bold weight
- **Body**: Calibri (sans-serif) - Regular weight
- **Characteristic**: Contrast between serif headings and clean body text

### Color Palette
| Color | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| McKinsey Blue | #2251FF | CTAs, highlights, accents |
| Dark Navy | #051C2C | Headings, primary text |
| Mid Gray | #666666 | Captions, secondary text |
| White | #FFFFFF | Background |

### Design Principles
- Bold, confident typography
- Sharp corners (0px border-radius)
- Large, impactful heading sizes
- Clean lines and precise spacing
- Executive presence

## Best Used For
- Strategy presentations
- Executive summaries
- Board presentations
- Business cases
- C-suite communications
- Management recommendations

## Style Specifications
```
H1: Georgia 52pt, #051C2C (Dark Navy), Bold
H2: Georgia 32pt, #051C2C, Bold
H3: Georgia 20pt, #051C2C, Bold
Body: Calibri 11pt, #051C2C
Caption: Calibri 9pt, #666666
```

## Document Characteristics
- Clean line table styling
- Sharp corners throughout
- Large, commanding headings
- Professional, authoritative tone
- CTA and highlight accent usage
