# Runwal Enterprises Brand Guide
## Complete Visual Identity Reference

**Heritage**: Since 1978

---

## Executive Summary

Runwal Enterprises maintains a **sophisticated, heritage-focused brand identity** established in 1978, built on a unified visual system that balances consistency with creative flexibility. The guidelines encompass logo usage, comprehensive color palettes (primary gold #B88815 and black #222222), typography standards, iconography systems, and application rules across all touchpoints.

---

## Typography Standards

### Font Families

| Purpose | Font | Style |
|---------|------|-------|
| **Headings** | Georgia | Serif, Bold - Elegance and tradition |
| **Body Text** | Calibri | Sans-serif, Regular - High readability |
| **Logo Monogram** | Custom Serif | Stylized "R" in structured square frame |

### Type Hierarchy

| Level | Size | Color | Weight | Usage |
|-------|------|-------|--------|-------|
| **H1** | 36pt | #B88815 (Gold) | Bold | Page titles, major sections |
| **H2** | 28pt | #222222 (Black) | Bold | Section headers |
| **H3** | 18pt | #222222 (Black) | Bold | Sub-sections |
| **Body** | 11pt | #222222 (Black) | Regular | General content |
| **Caption** | 9pt | #696B6D (Grey) | Regular | Footnotes, disclaimers |

### Case Usage
- Headlines in title case or uppercase for impact
- Body text in sentence case

## Table Styling

### Header Row
- **Background**: Runwal Gold `#B88815`
- **Text**: White `#FFFFFF`, Bold
- **Border**: Sharp corners (0 radius)

### Data Rows
- **Alternating**: Off-White `#FFFCF7` and Cream `#FBF8F3`
- **Text**: Black `#222222`
- **Style**: Elegant, minimal borders

## Style Specifications Summary

```
H1: Georgia 36pt, #B88815 (Runwal Gold), Bold
H2: Georgia 28pt, #222222 (Runwal Black), Bold
H3: Georgia 18pt, #222222 (Runwal Black), Bold
Body: Calibri 11pt, #222222 (Runwal Black)
Caption: Calibri 9pt, #696B6D (Grey 01)

Table Headers: Gold #B88815 background, White text, Bold
Table Data: Black #222222, alternating Off-White/Cream rows
Icons: Always in brand gold #B88815
Border Radius: 0 (sharp corners throughout)
```

---

## Brand Don'ts

- No distortion (stretching vertically/horizontally)
- No rotation at any angle
- No typography changes or layout alterations
- No special effects (shadows, gradients, textures)
- No color modifications from approved palette
- No placement on busy or low-contrast backgrounds
- No unapproved fonts
