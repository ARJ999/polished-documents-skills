# Runwal Enterprises Brand Guide

## Overview
Runwal Enterprises is a premium real estate heritage brand established in 1978, built on a sophisticated visual identity that balances tradition with modern excellence. The brand symbolizes trust, quality, and sophistication in the real estate industry.

## Visual Identity

### Typography
- **Headings**: Georgia (serif) - Bold weight for heritage elegance
- **Body**: Calibri (sans-serif) - Clean, professional readability
- **Characteristic**: Elegant serif headings paired with modern sans-serif body

### Color Palette

| Color | Hex | RGB | Usage |
|-------|-----|-----|-------|
| Runwal Gold | #B88815 | R:184 G:136 B:21 | Primary brand color, H1 headings, accents |
| Runwal Black | #222222 | R:34 G:34 B:34 | H2/H3 headings, body text |
| Off-White | #FFFCF7 | R:255 G:252 B:247 | Background |
| Medium Grey | #696B6D | R:105 G:107 B:109 | Secondary text, captions |
| Runwal Blue | #7AB8E4 | R:122 G:184 B:228 | Subtle accents |
| Runwal Brown | #48372D | R:72 G:55 B:45 | Depth elements |

### Design Principles
- **Heritage**: Established 1978, communicates trust and longevity
- **Sophistication**: Premium, elegant appearance
- **Quality**: Superior standards in every detail
- **Clarity**: Clean, readable, accessible design
- Sharp corners (0 border radius) for authority

## Best Used For
- Property brochures and marketing materials
- Investor reports and financial documentation
- Corporate presentations
- Premium real estate documentation
- Heritage and legacy documents
- Executive communications

## Style Specifications
```
H1: Georgia 36pt, #B88815 (Gold), Bold
H2: Georgia 28pt, #222222 (Black), Bold
H3: Georgia 18pt, #222222 (Black), Bold
Body: Calibri 11pt, #222222
Caption: Calibri 9pt, #696B6D
```

## Document Characteristics
- Elegant table style with gold accents
- Headers and highlights accent usage
- Premium, heritage aesthetic
- Sharp corners throughout
- Professional authority
- Trust and excellence messaging
